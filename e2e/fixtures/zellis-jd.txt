Director, Strategic & Enterprise Accounts
Zellis | Remote (UK) | Permanent

About Zellis
Zellis is the UK and Ireland's leading provider of payroll and HR solutions, trusted by over 1,000 organisations including a third of the FTSE 100. Our software powers the payroll for millions of employees across major enterprise accounts.

The Role
We are looking for a Director of Strategic & Enterprise Accounts to own and grow a portfolio of our most significant customers. This is a senior individual contributor role with full P&L accountability for a £multi-million ARR book of business. You will be responsible for retention, expansion, and executive relationship management across Zellis's top-tier enterprise accounts.

Key Responsibilities
- Own a portfolio of 15–25 strategic enterprise accounts with combined ARR of £8M–£15M
- Develop and execute account growth strategies to achieve net revenue retention > 110%
- Build and maintain C-suite and board-level relationships within each account
- Lead complex commercial negotiations for multi-year enterprise renewals and expansions
- Collaborate with Customer Success, Professional Services, and Product to deliver an exceptional enterprise experience
- Accurately forecast quarterly and annual revenue from your portfolio in Salesforce
- Represent the voice of the customer internally to influence roadmap and product prioritisation
- Travel to customer sites as required across the UK

What We're Looking For
- 8+ years of enterprise B2B SaaS sales or account management experience
- Proven track record of managing and growing £1M+ ARR accounts
- Experience selling into or managing relationships within HR, Finance, or Payroll buyer personas at enterprise scale
- Demonstrated ability to navigate complex multi-stakeholder enterprise organisations
- Strong commercial acumen — able to construct and negotiate multi-year enterprise agreements
- Experience with Salesforce CRM for pipeline management and forecasting
- Excellent executive presence and communication skills
- Degree-level education preferred (Business, Finance, or related field)
- Prior experience in the HCM, payroll, or HR technology sector is a significant advantage

What We Offer
- £90,000–£110,000 base salary DOE
- Uncapped OTE (additional 30–40% at target)
- 25 days holiday + bank holidays
- Private healthcare
- Enhanced pension
- Remote-first with occasional UK travel
